About Coca-Cola

Coca-Cola is a global leader in the beverage industry, offering a variety of refreshing drinks. Founded in 1886, Coca-Cola has become one of the most iconic brands in the world, known for its timeless and refreshing taste. Our mission is to refresh the world and make a difference, one drink at a time.

With over 500 brands in more than 200 countries, Coca-Cola is committed to innovation, sustainability, and community impact. We aim to provide high-quality beverages that bring happiness to people everywhere.
